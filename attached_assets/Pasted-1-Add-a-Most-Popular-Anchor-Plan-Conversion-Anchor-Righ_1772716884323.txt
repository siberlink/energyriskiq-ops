For EnergyRiskIQ, that should be:

serious users choose it

What to change

Highlight the Trader plan.

3️⃣ Introduce “Loss Aversion” in the Free Plan

Right now the free plan simply lists features.

But behavioral psychology shows people convert more when they feel they are missing something valuable.

Add a section like:

Free plan limitations

Example:

Free Plan Limitations

• No historical risk analysis
• No volatility forecasting
• No cross-market overlays
• No advanced scenario modelling

Then add a line:

Upgrade to unlock the full EnergyRiskIQ intelligence engine.
Why this works

Humans react more strongly to loss than gain.

So instead of thinking:

“Should I upgrade?”

They start thinking:

“What am I missing?”

This increases upgrade rates significantly.
