My strongest recommendation

I would not market this as a price prediction tool.

I would market it as an oil market decision-support system.

The home page should say something like:

"Explore how changes in geopolitical risk, financial markets, European gas stress, LNG dynamics, and supply conditions could influence Brent over the next 24 hours, 72 hours, and 7 days."

That positioning is both more credible and much harder for competitors to copy. They can calculate a regression between Brent and VIX; they cannot easily replicate a platform that integrates GERI Live, EERI, EGSI, gas storage, LNG, TTF, intelligence alerts, and intraday market data into a coherent scenario engine. That's where EnergyRiskIQ has a genuine competitive advantage.
