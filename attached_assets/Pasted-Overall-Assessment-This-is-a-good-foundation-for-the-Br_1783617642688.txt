Overall Assessment

This is a good foundation for the Brent Intelligence Scenario Engine™.

Strong points:

Professional institutional look.
Good live market environment section.
Multiple forecast horizons.
Scenario parameters are clear.
Driver attribution is valuable.
Historical analogs are excellent.
AI interpretation adds premium feel.
Saved scenarios and referral program are useful.

Main weakness:

The page has many good elements, but the workflow is not yet fully guided. A user should instantly understand:

What am I testing?
What changed?
What is the forecast?
Why?
What should I watch next?
1. Improve the Top Header

Current header is clean, but I would add a clear product positioning line under the title.

Add:

Institutional Brent scenario modelling using GERI Live, VIX, supply shocks, European gas stress, and historical analogs.

This explains why premium exists.

Also add a small status line:

Last updated: 09 July 2026, 16:49 UTC

You already show it lower, but it should be visible near the top.

2. Improve “Current Market Environment”

This section is good. I would add one extra card:

Market Regime

Example:

Elevated geopolitical risk / moderate volatility

This gives the user a fast interpretation instead of only numbers.

Also consider color labels:

GERI Daily: Elevated

EERI: Moderate

This makes it easier to scan.

The referral program is useful, but it should not compete with the tool.
