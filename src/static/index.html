Europe Gas Stress Index (EGSI)

Daily measure of gas market stress and system vulnerability in Europe

EGSI tracks gas-specific stress signals across European markets. It combines market transmission signals (EGSI-M) with system-level indicators (EGSI-S) including gas storage levels, TTF pricing, and chokepoint vulnerabilities.

Used to monitor:

gas market stress storage vulnerabilities pipeline chokepoints TTF price volatility

Gas-Specific Focus

Dedicated to natural gas markets, pipelines, LNG terminals, and storage facilities.

Dual Index Family

EGSI-M tracks market signals while EGSI-S monitors system fundamentals.

Chokepoint Tracking

Monitors Ukraine transit, TurkStream, Norway pipelines, and major LNG terminals.

Live Data Integration

Incorporates real AGSI+ storage data and TTF gas pricing for system stress.
